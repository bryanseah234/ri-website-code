Important Notice For New Students Joining Us via JAE Exercise

Welcome to our Rafflesian Family!
 
We trust you are as excited to join us as we are to meet with you!  Here are some important information for you to take note of for the first day of school:
 
1. REPORTING INFORMATION
Date:  5 Feb 2020 (Wednesday)
Time:  7.30am
Venue:  Block J, Performing Arts Centre
 
• Please report in your secondary school uniform.  As you will be having different briefings, you may wish to bring along a light jacket.
• You will be issued with new thermometer.
• Depending on the optional briefings you may want to attend, your first day with us should end between 2pm and 5pm.
 
2. IMPORTANT NOTICE ON nCoV MEASURES
We know you will not want to miss the first day of school but your wellbeing is of the utmost importance to us.
 
• If you are unwell, please see a doctor and recover fully before you join us.  Simply drop us an email at [email protected] to inform us that you are unable to report as you are on medical leave.
 
• If you have returned from Mainland China on 22 Jan or later, in accordance with the current health advisory, you should not report to school on 5 Feb 2020. Please contact us at [email protected] or 64199231 and we will issue you with a Leave of Absence (LOA).

Founded in 1823, RI is a pre-tertiary institution that aims to provide all her students with an education that enables them to excel in their many passions and pursuits.
